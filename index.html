My Writing Background

I have had an affinity for storytelling ever since I had the ability to write and speak. I admire the process of gathering information and transforming it into a well-designed report on a topic.

I want to partake in disseminating factual and engaging news to inform and inspire the public.


As a recent graduate from Concordia University in the Journalism program, I have had the opportunity to develop my reporting, researching and writing skills.

I have taken research methods courses that taught me how to use databases such as JSTOR, ProQuest and EBSCO, prompting me to further my research when writing an article.

Although my degree focuses on the written aspect of journalism, I have taken courses that enabled me to learn the basics of audiovisual journalism. I learned the functions of an HTML and HDMI camera as well as how to navigate publishing software applications such as InDesign, Photoshop, and Final Cut Pro.

Shortly after graduating University, I completed one internship at CJAD and another at Rank Media. Both internships taught me proper time management and good research skills.


I now volunteer my time writing for the Tyndale St-Georges seasonal newsletter.
