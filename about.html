Urban Lady

I was born and raised in the fascinatingly eclectic city of Montreal, Quebec. I don’t mean to be bias but I adore my city!
From the numerous flamboyant orange and white construction cones scattered around the streets to our many festivals celebrating art, music and culture, there will NEVER be a dull moment here.
As a writer, I always feel inspired by what my city has to offer. Every week, I hit up a new chic café in the downtown area and fervidly write poetry about -literally- anything and everything around me.
I am an urban lady ( who also sometimes if not rarely likes the countryside) and always will be.

Also, my Instagram poetry page, _crazychildwrite_, is where I unleash my inner crazy introspective child. You should check it out, it’s pretty nice.
